# Privacy Policy for Unit Converter

**Last Updated: February 8, 2026**

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the Unit Converter mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

**Unit Converter does not collect any personal information.** The app functions completely offline and does not require internet access or user accounts.

### What We Do NOT Collect:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports with personal data
- Contact information
- Photos or media files
- Any other personal data

## How the App Works

- All conversions are performed locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No user accounts or registration required
- No internet connection needed for functionality
- All calculations happen in real-time on your device

## Data Storage

- The app may store user preferences (like theme settings) locally on your device
- No personal data is stored
- No data is backed up to cloud services
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

## Third-Party Services

- The app does not integrate with any third-party services
- No advertising networks
- No analytics services
- No social media integrations
- No external APIs

## Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for children of all ages as it:

- Does not collect any personal information
- Contains no social features
- Has no in-app purchases
- Contains no advertisements
- Does not require internet access
